Weekcase Metric Library

Weekcase Metric Library (WML) is an open-source library of standardized key business metrics. WML includes clear, unambiguous definitions for selected metrics, and specifications on how to extract them from selected systems.

WML exists to reduce ambiguity around common business metrics by providing a stable, versioned viewpoint, enabling consistent reporting and analysis across different tools, platforms, and teams.

Weekcase is developed and maintained by Weekcase, a company building trusted business metrics infrastructure.
